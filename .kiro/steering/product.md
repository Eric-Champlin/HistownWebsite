## Mission
HisTown is a Christian-based dance and music studio website designed to inspire families and students through faith-driven creativity and movement. The goal is to provide clear program info, class schedules, event registration, and inspirational content aligned with Christian values.

## Target Audience
- Families and students interested in dance, performance, and faith-based programs
- Visitors looking for studio information, class signups, or upcoming events

## Key Goals
- Refresh and modernize the design from the old HisTown prototype using a clean, React/TypeScript and Tailwind-based layout
- Improve responsiveness, accessibility, and visual flow
- Highlight the studio's Christian mission while keeping design modern and welcoming
- Simplify navigation and encourage registration or contact

## Tone & Style
Warm, encouraging, faith-centered but not overly formal. Focus on clarity, community, and inspiration. Use uplifting imagery, soft gradients, and movement to reflect the creative nature of the studio.
